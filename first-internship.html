POSITION | Innovation Lab Coordinator II

ORGANIZATION | Library Division of the Neighborhood Services Department at Manatee County Government

PROJECT DESCRIPTION | Find ways to recruit, mentor, and train makerspace volunteers. Also oversee the lab; discovering efficiencies and mentoring projects, so as to develop a basis for future makerspaces in the library system.

PROJECT OUTCOME | A total of 8 volunteers were recruited for the makerspace, which was double the goal amount of 4. Rebekah was also able to successfully contact and connect with 10 local organizations in Manatee County. And in collaboration with the Bilingual Volunteer Coordinator, Rebekah created 4 informational videos and 4 promotional flyers for the Central Library. TOOLS & PROGRAMS UTILIZED | Microsoft (MS) Office 365, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe InDesign.
